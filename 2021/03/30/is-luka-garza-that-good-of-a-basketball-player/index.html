Is Luka Garza that good of a basketball player?

2021-03-30

I was doing a little bit of research over the potential draft prospects and I noticed that Luke Garza wasn’t ranked very high. He is projected to be in the late-first to mid-second round of draft picks. Garza plays as a center for the Iowa Hawkeyes in the Big Ten Conference. How is it possible that Luka Garza is currently ranked no. 1 in Men’s College Basketball but won’t be a first round NBA draft pick? Hopefully by looking at his statistics we can get a feel for what he’s missing as a player.
